Privacy Policy for Challenge Dice

Last updated: March 25, 2026

JRH Studios (“we”, “our”, or “us”) built Challenge Dice as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information Stored on Your Device

Challenge Dice stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device unless you use the online multiplayer feature:

  • Game preferences — settings such as auto-roll, haptic feedback, and layout preferences
  • Game saves — in-progress game state so you can resume later
  • High scores — your top scores categorized by game mode
  • Achievements — unlocked achievements, win streaks, and games-played counters
  • Online player profile — your chosen display name and color for online games

Information Collected for Online Multiplayer

If you choose to play online multiplayer games, the following data is sent to and stored on Google Firebase servers:

  • Anonymous user ID — a randomly generated identifier created by Firebase Anonymous Authentication; no email, password, or personal information is required
  • Display name — the name you choose to enter when creating or joining an online game
  • Game data — your chosen player color, dice rolls, scorecard marks, scores, and connection status during an active game
  • Timestamps — game creation and last activity times, used to clean up inactive games

Online game data is associated only with your anonymous ID and is used solely to facilitate real-time multiplayer gameplay.

Information Collected for Leaderboards and Achievements

Challenge Dice uses Google Play Games Services (GPGS) to provide global leaderboards and achievements. If you are signed in to Google Play Games on your device, the following data may be collected and shared with Google:

  • Play Games player ID — your Google Play Games profile identifier
  • Display name — your Google Play Games profile name, visible on leaderboards
  • Scores — your best score and lifetime cumulative score, submitted to global leaderboards
  • Achievement progress — which achievements you have unlocked

This data is managed by Google and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. Sign-in to Google Play Games is automatic on supported devices but can be managed through your device’s Google Play Games settings. You can play the game without signing in; leaderboards and achievement syncing will simply be unavailable.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage telemetry
  • We do not use crash reporting services
  • We do not display ads or use advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive device data
  • We do not require or collect email addresses, passwords, or real names
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes

Third-Party Services

Challenge Dice uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Realtime Database) — used for online multiplayer. Firebase’s privacy policy can be found at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Games Services — used for global leaderboards and achievements. Google’s privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Anonymous Authentication assigns a random user ID without collecting personal information. Firebase Realtime Database stores online game state data as described above. Google Play Games Services uses your existing Google Play Games profile for leaderboards and achievements.

Data Retention

  • Local data is stored on your device until you clear the app’s data or uninstall the app.
  • Online game data on Firebase is temporary. Completed and inactive games are cleaned up automatically. Anonymous user accounts may be removed by Firebase after extended periods of inactivity per Google’s retention policies.

Children’s Privacy

Challenge Dice does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function. Online multiplayer uses only anonymous authentication and a user-chosen display name.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at JRHStudiosApp@gmail.com.
