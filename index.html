How to Play

1

Roll 5 Dice

All players share the same roll each round — it's what you do with them that counts.

2

Form 2 Pairs

Pick 4 dice to make two pairs. Their sums are marked on your scorecard rows 2–12.

3

Mark the 5th Die

Your remaining die is marked on the right scorecard, rows 1–6.

4

Score Big

Push past the penalty zone into scoring territory for massive multipliers — up to 10x!

Features

🎲

1–4 Players

Local pass-and-play with optional AI opponents at Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty.

🌐

Online Multiplayer

Real-time games with a lobby system, simultaneous scoring, and leave/rejoin support.

🎓

Interactive Tutorial

Learn as you play with a guided walkthrough you can replay anytime from Settings.

🎨

Player Color Themes

Each player gets a unique color — blue, red, green, or purple.

🔍

Live Scorecard Preview

See exactly how your scorecard will change before you commit. Pinch to zoom, tap for details.

🎉

Confetti & Celebrations

Satisfying animations, haptic feedback, and a confetti finish when the game ends.
