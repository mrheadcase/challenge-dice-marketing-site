How to Play

1

Roll 5 Dice

All players share the same roll each round — it's what you do with them that counts.

2

Form 2 Pairs

Pick 4 dice to make two pairs. Their sums are marked on your scorecard rows 2–12.

3

Mark the 5th Die

Your remaining die is marked on the right scorecard, rows 1–6.

4

Score Big

Push past the penalty zone into scoring territory for massive multipliers — up to 10x!
